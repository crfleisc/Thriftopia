About

THRIFTOPIA was established in 2013 with its very first location... Calgary.

In December 2017, a space located at 214-62 Ave SE was secured. A 6000 sq/ft warehouse. We are really excited to offer our unique blanket pricing to the Calgary public on a large scale.

Our goal with Thriftopia and the whole reason we started this organization was to direct dollars to charities which we hold close to our hearts. Most weekends, we have volunteers trying to raise money for their causes working the floors and stocking inventory.
